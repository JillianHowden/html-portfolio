Jill's all-time favorite movies

My top 3 ranking films

Love Actually

This is my favorite of all time. I watch it every holiday and wrap presents to it

Black Panther

Both Black Panther and Wakanda Forever rank as two more. Comedy, action and love are all great elements that tie these movies together

Avatar

Both Avatar and The Way of Water are beautifully done and show a striking example of humans and there lack of respect as well as the need to look toward how we can all work together.
